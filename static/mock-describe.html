Intention is the force that brings an organization together. It is the union of our mental models that manifests as our intention. It is our preference to some outcomes and not others, and when intentions within us are less aligned, we experience loss of meaning. It is a commitment or resolution of one sort or another, and is proportional to how well we can navigate the multitude of our internal intentions.
